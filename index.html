These scenarios are very common. There are many reasons people want a
career change. Some people will spend a lot of time trying to make sense of
where they fit into the workforce and become more confused about the options.
They may find something that interests them but can't take the next step.

Many people seek the help of a career guidance counsellor or a career coach,
while others use a number of online career tests. First of all let's look at career
coaching.
